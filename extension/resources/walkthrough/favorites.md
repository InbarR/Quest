# Save Your Favorites

Build a personal library of frequently used queries.

## Adding to Favorites

There are several ways to save a query:

- Click the **star icon** in the editor toolbar
- Right-click and select **Add to Favorites**
- Use **Ctrl+Shift+F** keyboard shortcut

## Organizing Favorites

Your favorites are automatically organized by mode:

- **KQL Favorites** - Your Kusto queries
- **ADO Favorites** - Your work item queries
- **OQL Favorites** - Your Outlook queries

## Using Favorites

Click any favorite to instantly load it into the editor. Your favorites appear in the **Favorites** panel in the sidebar.

## Tips

- Give queries descriptive names when saving
- Favorites sync across VS Code sessions
- Use favorites for complex queries you run often
